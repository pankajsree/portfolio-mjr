The NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, AGARTALA shortened to NIT, AGARTALA or NITA is a technology-oriented institute of higher education established by India's Ministry of Human Resource Developement Government of India in Agartala, India. It was basically founded as the Tripura Engineering College (TEC) in the year 1965 and declared as the National Institute of Technology (NIT) in 2006. The Institute is one of the premier technical institutes in the North-Eastern India recently ranking 92 among the engineering colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2018. The Institute offers nine four-year undergraduate programmes in Engineering. The Institute is 4 km off National Highway (NH44) and 24 km from the capital city of Agartala.

Mechanical Engineering is that discipline that applies engineering physics, engineering mathematics and material science principles to design, analyze,manufacture and maintain mechanical systems. It is one of the oldest and broadest of all the Engineering disciplines. Mechanical Engineering emerges as a field during the Industrial Revolution in Europe in the 18th century. Usually known as the 'King' of all the engineering branches, the field has continually evolved to incorporate advancements in various arenas. The Mechanical Engineering Department in NIT, AGARTALA is in no way behind providing several theorical and practical workshop oriented opportunities to its students.
